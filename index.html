Meet Yvonne

Love, Trust & Respect.

‚ùù

Those are the basic principles my training teaches both humans and animals. I am a second generation dog trainer and animal behaviourist. My father was a dog trainer for the United States Army, based in Germany. I grew up with a father who taught me all he knew and tuned me into a deep connection with animals, humans and music. It was natural to follow in his footsteps and let my love and connection with animals take me around the world.

Dog Training.

I completed a series of certified dog training through the United States Army, based in Germany.

I further qualified as an animal behaviourist specialising not only in dogs, but also cats and horses. I completed my diploma in Oxford England.

I have been practicing as a professional dog trainer and animal behaviourist for the past 35 years.

I have trained dogs and animal handlers for the police, military and private clients.

Animal Rescue.

My work has taken me across the world, from the United Kingdom, Germany and across Europe to Dubai and Nigeria.

My passion for working with street animals and assisting animal rescue societies across the world, brought me to South Africa.

Since 2019, I have trained domestic animals and assisted HAWS (Hermanus Animal Welfare Society) and Rottweiler Rescue with assessing and training pets for their new homes.
